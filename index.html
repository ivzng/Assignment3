GBDA 202: Re-Brand It! – First Aid Beauty

For this branding project, I reimagined the skincare company First Aid Beauty, aiming to simplify its loud and chaotic visual identity while preserving key brand elements. I began by developing a strategic foundation through competitive analysis and a refreshed mission statement. From there, I created a modern visual identity that focuses on clean, typography-based branding—an intentional choice inspired by trends in the skincare industry. Rather than relying on an icon logo, I used refined type treatments to convey trust, clarity, and care, aligning with the brand’s core values while giving it a more cohesive and elevated look.
